MesoMerchant
Description

The purpose of this RPG is to enrich the history education of elementary children attending the sixth grade. This is achieved through in-game elements such NPC dialogs, objects, etc. Players will travel back in time to the Phoenician era and interact with an environment very different from their own. This games offers a different method of educating children while also being entertaining.

Prototype

MesoMerchant currently runs on version 1.6.5 of Solarus which can be downloaded from the website found below.
